Predicting Airbnb Rental Cost in 4 Major U.S. Cities

Skills: Hypothesis Testing, Statistical Testing, JMP

Objectives: Created a predictive model on the rental fee charged by Airbnb based on a number of housing/property features during New Year Day across 4 major U.S. cities.

Approach: Collected 100 data points on Airbnb website for 4 cities from December 31 to January 1, 2022. Then fit the data into linear, multiple and logistic regression in JMP Software. Trained the data and performed a model selection based on R-Squared values.

Results: Multiple Linear Regression performed better relative to other two. Some features such as the number of guests, cleaning fee, service fee behaved as expected since the price per night at Airbnb increased when these factors went up. One interest finding was that a free parking significantly bumped up the price of Airbnb because of its location in metropolitan area.
