Teaching Limit Concepts Using Visualization

Skills: Research and Development, Survey

Objectives: Researched teaching strategies to help struggling college students to understand calculus concepts. Main area of focus was limit.

Approach: Interviewed college or high school students on their thought process when solving limit problems after they were primed to watch a 10-minute video of either the traditional teaching method or Rule of Convenience.
